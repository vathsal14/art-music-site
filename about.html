About Us

Welcome to Artbeat Institute of Drawing, Painting and Music, a creative haven where art and music come alive!

At Artbeat, we believe that everyone has an Artist within them. Our mission is to nurture creativity, inspire self-expression, and provide a joyful learning experience through Drawing, Painting, and Music classes for all ages and skill levels.

Whether you're a beginner exploring your first brushstroke, an aspiring Musician discovering melodies, or an experienced Artist refining your craft, our passionate instructors are here to guide you every step of the way.

We offer a supportive and engaging environment where imagination flourishes and talents grow.
